Treatment Facility Search

Select a category and service to view all facilities that provide your query. Click on any marker to get the name of the selected facility. Click, drag, or zoom on the map to navigate to an area of your choosing. Alternatively, enter an address or city in the search bar below the map to focus on the selected area.

About This App

This map interface displays all facilites listed on SAMHSA's 2022 National Directory of Drug and Alcohol Abuse Treatment Facilities. It is a listing of federal, state, local, and private facilities that are licensed, certified, or otherwise approved for inclusion in the Directory by their State Substance Abuse Agencies, and those facilities that responded to the 2021 National Substance Use and Mental Health Services Survey.

The goal of this project is to provide a unique tool for searching and finding specific treatments, services, or facilites, as well as to educate and provide additional information on the facilities themselves and the geospatial distribution of treatment centers in the United States.
